## Farmer's market or everyone's market?

Going to a farmer's market is the best way to buy local, but we can find more than just delicious fruits and vegetables. Creatives of all mediums set up shop to sell earrings, paintings, crochet animals, and more. The traditional farmer's market has given people a chance to sell their wares and expand beyond Etsy and Facebook marketplace.
