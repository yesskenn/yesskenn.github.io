## LOCAL MUSIC SHOP REDESIGN + MARKETING

* * *

## OVERVIEW

Build a WordPress Site for a Local Business with Multimedia Marketing Assets

* * *

### PROJECT TYPE: WEB DESIGN • BRAND DESIGN• SOCIAL MEDIA

This project was created for a Northern California-based woodwind instrument repair and rental shop, Got-A-Gig Music Shop. The goal was to design a modern, visually engaging, and user-friendly website that showcased the shop's expertise, services, and dedication to quality craftsmanship.

Key features include—

#### Content Strategy

Writing and structuring informative "About" and "Services" pages to connect with the target audience. Creating graphics for social media.

#### Visually Rich Hero Section

Highlighting the timeless beauty of woodwind instruments through high-quality imagery.

#### Responsive Design

Ensuring seamless usability across devices, from desktop to tablet.

#### Clear Navigation

Providing easy access to information about repairs, rentals, and services.
