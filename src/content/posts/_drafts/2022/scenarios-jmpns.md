The purpose of these scenarios was to imagine being a user navigating through the site and trying to find information or complete a task. Thinking through a problem is a big part of being able to solve those problem and eventually articulate design decisions. It is important to reason through a problem, or bring it back from abstraction, and then defend why you came to certain conclusions.
